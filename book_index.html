Why use BookWorm?

Reading a great book that one enjoys all the way through doesn't happen all the time, and, especially for those starting out, it can be difficult to know where to look to find one. This is where BookWorm comes in; it's an application that can help you find the perfect book to get back into reading, whether you're young or old, there are books out there for all kinds of people!
